Blue (Da Ba Dee)

"Blue (Da Ba Dee)" is a song by Italian band Eiffel 65. It is the lead single from their debut album Europop. The text is a play on words: it describes a hero who lives in a blue world and experiences “blue” (sad) feelings.

[Intro]

Yo, listen up, here's a story
About a little guy, that lives in a blue world
And all day and all night and everything he sees is just blue
Like him inside and outside
Blue his house with a blue little window
And a blue corvette and everything is blue for him
And himself and everybody around
Cause he ain't got nobody to listen(To listen, to listen, to listen)
